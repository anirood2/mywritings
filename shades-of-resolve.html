Shades of Resolve

A Novel in Seven Chapters

✦
Literary Fiction Principles Integrity Conflict
❝

He was liked by everyone — and known by no one. For years, Daniel Sharma mistook keeping the peace for living with integrity. But there is a profound difference between the man who makes everyone comfortable and the man who does what is right — and that difference, when it finally surfaces, changes everything.

A story about the quiet courage it takes to stop being nice, and start being good.
